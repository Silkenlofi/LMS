What we offer

Sterilised Substrates
Pre-sterilised and ready to use, our substrates are ideal for colonising mushroom cultures with minimal contamination risk. Available in soy hull pellets, hardwood blends, and other nutrient-rich mixes.
Sterilised Grain
Premium-quality sterilised grain spawn, including rye, millet, and wheat. Each bag is pressure-sterilised and sealed, providing the perfect environment for strong mycelial growth.
All-in-One Grow Bags
Convenient grow bags that combine sterilised grain and substrate in one unit. Perfect for beginners or low-maintenance grows — just inoculate, incubate, and fruit directly from the bag.
Liquid Cultures
Meaty, rich, and naturally nootropic, Lion’s Mane is the showstopper. Its texture mimics crab or lobster, making it a favourite for plant-based dishes and experimental mains. We grow it slow to bring out that signature density and clean, mild flavour.
Mycology Equipment
Delicate yet bold, our oyster mushrooms are grown for colour, character, and consistency. Blue oysters bring a deep umami kick, pinks add a hint of seafood sweetness, and yellows are light, nutty, and slightly citrusy.
